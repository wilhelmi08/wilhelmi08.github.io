Overview

I made this mod, so Lethal Company players can calculate precisely how much scrap they need to sell in the game.

Usage

You can use the command 'calc [wanted money]' in the in-game terminal to calculate how much scrap you need to sell achieve the value of money you want.

You can also use 'calc quota' to subtract the already earned money from your quota, 'calc help' for a list of commands and you can also use expressions in the command, e.g. 'calc 1500+400'.
